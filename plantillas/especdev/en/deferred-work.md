# Deferred Work

Work discovered during fixes, PoCs, or implementation that is OUTSIDE the current scope. Logged here to prevent scope creep and prioritized later.

## Format

### DW-NNN: [Short description]
- **Discovered in:** [Task/PoC/Fix where it was found]
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Estimated priority:** high | medium | low
- **Estimated effort:** < 1h | 1-4h | 1d | 1w+
- **Context:** [Why it came up and why it wasn't resolved now]
- **Status:** pending | planned | discarded
